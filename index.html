About Letters Drop

Letters Drop is a word game where letters fall from the top of the screen and you have to arrange them in rows in the bottom to make words.

Words of three letters will score one point, four-letter words will score 2 points, five-letter words will score 3 points, 6-letter words will score 4 points and 7-letter words will score 7 points and also clear the screen of all tiles.

Blank tiles will be displayed in the ‘next word’ column as ?. When it lands you will be given the opportunity to choose what letter it becomes. Choose wisely!

Words found will vanish from the grid and any letters that fall to make new words will score a x2 cascade multiplier. If another word is found you will score a x3 multiplier.

The game will speed up every minute, and will end when no letters can fall from the top of the grid.

Only words across from left to right are allowed.

All words must be in the English dictionary.

No names, brands and place names.

Letters Drop is designed as a brain training skill game to test speed, vocabulary and logical thinking.
